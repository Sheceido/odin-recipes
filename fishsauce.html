THE ODIN RECIPES

FISH SAUCE

Ingredients

  • - 1 Tablespoon fish sauce
  • - 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • - 3 Tablespoon water
  • - 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • - 1/2 a Lime, to taste

Steps:

  1. Combine ingredients!
  2. As in the picture, you can also add fresh chilli slices to spice things up.
