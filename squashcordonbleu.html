Cordon Bleu Squash

Ingredients

  • - One (3lbs) Butternut Squash
  • - Salt and pepper, to taste
  • - 1 Large Egg, beaten
  • - 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions (+ more for garnish)
  • - 10 ounces diced smoked ham
  • - 6 ounces Gruyere Cheese, diced
  • - 2 ounces Gruyere Cheese, grated
  • - 1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard

Steps:

  1. Prheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Cut butternut squash half lengthwise, starting at stem-end; scoop out seeds. Slice off 1/8th of an inch from round side to ensure stability when baking 'meat'-side up
  3. Transfer squash onto baking sheet, place at center of oven and roast until tender, approximately 60-90 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and let sit until cool to handle. Increase oven temperature to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C)
  5. Use a spoon to scoop squash into a bowl, leaving about 1/2 an inch attached to skin for structure.
  6. Add salt, pepper, dijon mustard, and egg to squash. Mix and mash with spoon until smooth (or leave it chunky if desired). Stir in 1/4 cup green onions, ham, diced gruyere cheese - combine.
  7. Scoop mixture back into squash shells, distributing evenly, then add grated gruyere cheese on top.
  8. Bake in center of oven until cheese is browned (approximately 15 minutes).
  9. Transfer onto cutting board, top with green onions. Slice and serve!
